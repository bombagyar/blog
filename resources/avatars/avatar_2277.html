Onion.pet - Tor2Web Darknet Proxy

Tor Proxy

Onion.pet acts as a proxy between the Tor Darknet, also known as hidden services, and you on the regular, or clearnet. How does this work? Replace the .onion part of any Tor hidden service with ".onion.pet" and we'll proxy you directly to that hidden service.
